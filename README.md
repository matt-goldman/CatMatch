## A simple memory game, with cats

I made this game because my daughter loves cats. And I needed something to keep her occupied when work, watch TV, or play games without her needing my constant attention. It didn't work, because she absolutely loves showing me the cats and gets me to celebrate with her when she scores a point. But on the plus side, she loves the game! üòç
